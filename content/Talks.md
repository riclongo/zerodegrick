## 2025

- [New ATLAS Results from O+O and Ne+Ne Collisions: shedding light on QGP and small nuclei properties][def]: CERN seminar on behalf of ATLAS at the CERN Jamboree on results from the 2025 light ion run at the LHC, CERN, Switzerland; 16 September 2025;

- [Light ions at the LHC: early results from the Oxygen and Neon runs][ismd]: invited talk at the 53rd International Symposium on Multiparticle Dynamics, Corfu, Greece; 21-26 September 2025. 

- [Opportunities for Heavy Ion at ATLAS with the Phase-II upgrades and synergies with the EIC][nupecc]: invited talk at the Joint ECFA-NuPECC-APPEC Workshop on synergies between the EIC and the LHC, IFJ Pan, Krakow, Poland; 21-23 September 2025. 

- [Overview of jet results from the LHC][rhicum]: invited talk at the RHIC/AGS Annual User Meeting 2025, at
Brookhaven National Laboratory, NY, US; 23-25 May 2025.

- [Heavy Ions: how can future facilities answer the main open questions?][lhcp]: invited talk in the Upgrades and Future Projects parallel session of the 2025 LHC Physics Conference, Taipei, 5-9 May 2025. 

- [ATLAS Highlights][qm25]: plenary ATLAS experimental overview at the Quark Matter 2025 Conference, Frankfurt, 6-12 April 2025.

- [Overview of recent ATLAS jet results in Heavy Ions][hj25]: invited talk at the first Hot Jets: Advancing the Understanding of High Temperature QCD with Jets workshop, Urbana, 8-10 January 2025. 

## 2024

- [A journey through the Early Universe with the ATLAS Experiment at CERN][isur24]: invited Seminar at the University of Illinois Scholars Undergraduate Research (ISUR) program, Urbana, 11 December 2024. 

- [Hard-jet correlations in small and large systems][hp24]: invited plenary talk at the 12th International Conference on Hard and Electromagnetic Probes of High-Energy Nuclear Collisions (Hard Probes 2024), Nagasaki, 22-27 September 2024. 

- [Synergies between the ATLAS Heavy Ion and the EIC Physics Programs][int24]: invited talk at the INT Heavy Ion Physics in the EIC Era Workshop, Institute for Nuclear Theory, Seattle, 18-24 August 2024. 

- [ATLAS status and plans for studies in hadronic p+A collisions][pA24]: talk at the Physics with high-luminosity proton-nucleus collisions at the LHC Workshop, CERN, 4-5 July 2024. 

- [Status of the COMPASS TMD program][solid24]: invited talk at the 2024 SoLID Opportunities and Challenges of Nuclear Physics at the Luminosity Frontier, Argonne National Laboratory, 17-22 June 2024. 

- [Studying the emergence of Hot and Cold QCD phenomena using Jets][uconn24]: invited Nuclear Physics Seminar at the University of Connecticut, Storrs, 18th March 2024. 

- [Studying the emergence of Hot and Cold QCD phenomena in Heavy Ions at the ATLAS Experiment][bo24]: invited High-Energy Physics Seminar at the University of Bologna, 27th February 2024. 

- [Studying the emergence of Hot and Cold QCD phenomena using Jets][ku24]: invited Physics Colloquium at the University of Kansas Physics and Astronomy Department, Lawrence (KS), 29th January 2024. 


## 2023 

- [Probing proton size fluctuations and nuclear effects via dijet measurements in heavy ion collisions at the LHC][uiuc23]: invited Nuclear Physics Seminar at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Urbana, 4th December 2023.

- [Radiation-hard Particle Detectors and Calorimeters in Nuclear and High Energy Physics][npre23]: invited lecture at Nuclear, Plasma & Radiological Engineering 452, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 13 November 2023. 

- [Investigation of initial state effects in p+Pb collisions at ATLAS via measurement of centrality dependence of dijet yields][qm23]: poster at the XXXth International Conference on Ultra-relativistic Nucleus-Nucleus Collisions, Quark Matter 2023, Houston (TX), 3-9 September 2023.

- [Investigation of initial state effects in p+Pb collisions at ATLAS via measurement of centrality dependence of dijet yields][is23]: talk at the VII-th International Conference on the Initial Stages of High-Energy Nuclear Collisions : Initial Stages 2023, Copenhagen, 19-23 June 2023. 

- [ATLAS Forward Detectors in the HL-LHC era][fwd23]: talk at the LHC Foward Physics Meeting, CERN, 9 June 2023

- [Investigation of initial state effects in p+Pb collisions at ATLAS via measurement of both top quark and dijet production][hp23]: talk at the 11th International Conference on Hard and Electromagnetic Probes of High-Energy Nuclear Collisions (Hard Probes 2023), Aschaffenburg, 26-31 March 2023.
