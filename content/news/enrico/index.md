The ATLAS Torino group congratulates its younger member, now Dott. Enrico Peter Grande for successfully defending his bachelor's thesis today, July 23rd 2026! Enrico's thesis was focused on the study of cold nuclear matter effects on the dijet asymmetry measurement recently carried out by ATLAS in O+O and Ne+Ne collisions. He gave an impeccable presentation, nicely illustrating the hard work he did to assess nPDF effects on the ATLAS dijet asymmetry in light ions. **Congratulations, Dott. Grande!**
